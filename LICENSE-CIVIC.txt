This license exists to ensure that early WCO code, schemas, and civic objects remain part of a shared global commons.

Redistribute modified or unmodified versions

Use the materials for civic, educational, research, or public-interest purposes

3. Reciprocity
If you publicly deploy, publish, or operate a modified version of the WCO substrate or its civic objects, you must:

Make your modifications publicly available under this same license

Clearly state what you changed

Not restrict others from using your changes

This ensures improvements flow back into the commons.

Claim exclusive ownership over the WCO name, civic objects, schemas, or substrate

Use the WCO name to imply official affiliation without permission
